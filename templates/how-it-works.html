Privacy FAQ

Do you store my photos?

No. We process as a stream and discard raw files. We store only embedded face data for matching.

What is embedded face data?

A numerical representation of a face, not the actual image. It cannot be used to reconstruct the photo.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can request deletion of your embedded data at any time.

Do you scrape the web?

No. We never crawl or scrape public internet sources.
